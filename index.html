Design Process

In each project, I follow a user-centered design process that begins with understanding user needs and ends with delivering polished, high-fidelity designs.

User Research


Conducting interviews, surveys, and usability studies to gain insights into user behavior and preferences.

Ideation and Concept Development


Brainstorming ideas, sketching concepts, and creating low-fidelity prototypes to explore design possibilities.

Prototyping and Iteration


Building interactive prototypes to test with users and gathering feedback to iterate on the design.

Usability Testing


Conducting usability tests to identify usability issues and validate design decisions before finalizing the design.
